RANG-E-ALFAZ

DARPAN

Identity — Reflection

Masoom Rang

Roots & Wonder

In the colours of innocence,
we wrote our first stories—
before the world taught us
what words should mean.

Jab Sheesha Dharakta Hai

Fragments

When the mirror cracks,
we see a hundred selves—
shattered by words
that were never ours.

Khoj

Searching

Letters to the self,
questions with no answers,
a journey inward—
through darkness, into light.

Sweekar

Acceptance

In forgiveness, we find wholeness,
in acceptance, we find peace—
the mirror repaired,
the reflection, finally ours.

Guests & Performers

Amara Rizvi

Poet

A voice that speaks of forgotten dreams and unspoken truths.

Rohit Sen

Reader

Words are his sanctuary, and every reading is a homecoming.

Nisha Kapoor

Singer

Her melodies carry the weight of untold stories and ancient echoes.

Imran Ali

Musician

In his hands, instruments become vessels of emotion and memory.

Your words matter. Your story is poetry.
